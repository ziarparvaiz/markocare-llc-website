Howard County is one of Maryland's most affluent and well-resourced counties — and that wealth of public infrastructure extends to programs and services for older adults and their families. If you are navigating care for a senior loved one in Howard County, knowing where to turn is half the battle.

This guide is a practical overview of the key resources available locally.

## Howard County Office on Aging and Independence (OAI)

The **Howard County Office on Aging and Independence** is the central hub for senior services in the county. Located in Columbia, OAI administers a broad range of programs including:

- **Information and Assistance:** Trained staff who can help families understand available services and navigate the care system
- **Care Management:** Professional care managers who assess needs and coordinate services for older adults at risk of institutionalization
- **Long-Term Care Ombudsman:** Advocates for residents in nursing facilities and assisted living communities
- **Caregiver Support Services:** Programs specifically designed to support family caregivers, including education, counseling, and referrals

OAI can be reached by phone or through Howard County's Aging Services portal. A call to their Information and Assistance line is a good first step for any family that does not know where to start.

## Senior Centers

Howard County operates several senior centers that provide socialization, programming, health promotion, and meals:

- **Bain Senior Center** (Columbia) — One of the largest in the county, offering fitness classes, arts programs, and a full lunch program
- **Dobbin Road Center** (Columbia)
- **Elkridge Senior Center**
- **East Columbia 50+ Center**

Senior centers are free or low-cost to attend and can significantly reduce isolation for older adults who are still living independently.

## Nutrition Programs

**Meals on Wheels** is available throughout Howard County and delivers hot, nutritious meals to homebound seniors who cannot prepare food themselves. The program also provides a daily wellness check by the delivery volunteer — an important safety net for seniors living alone.

Senior centers also offer congregate meals, where seniors can eat lunch in a social setting for a modest suggested donation.

## Transportation Services

Getting to medical appointments and daily activities is one of the most common challenges for older adults who can no longer drive. Howard County offers several options:

- **Howard Transit:** Fixed-route bus service with accessibility features and reduced fares for seniors
- **RideLink:** Paratransit service for individuals with disabilities that prevents them from using fixed-route transit
- **OAI Volunteer Driver Program:** Volunteer drivers for medical appointments and essential errands

Ride-sharing services such as Lyft and Uber also offer Senior Assist programs that can be managed by family members on behalf of older adults.

## Medicaid and Waiver Programs

Maryland offers several Medicaid waiver programs that can help offset the cost of home care for seniors who meet financial and functional eligibility criteria:

- **Community First Choice (CFC):** Provides personal assistance services and other home-based supports to Medicaid-eligible individuals
- **Community Personal Assistance Services (CPAS):** Funding for personal care and companion services in the home
- **Developmental Disabilities Administration (DDA) waivers:** For adults with developmental disabilities

Determining eligibility for these programs can be complex. Howard County OAI's care managers can help families understand eligibility and navigate the application process.

## Veterans Services

Howard County veterans may be eligible for care benefits through the **Department of Veterans Affairs**, including:

- **Aid and Attendance Pension:** A VA pension benefit specifically for veterans and surviving spouses who need help with daily activities
- **Veteran-Directed Care Program:** Allows veterans to direct their own home care services

The Howard County Veterans Services Office can provide guidance on available benefits.
