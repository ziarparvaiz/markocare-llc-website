Carroll County's blend of rural landscape and suburban communities creates a distinctive character — one that requires a home care agency willing to go the distance, literally and figuratively. MarkoCare serves Carroll County families in Westminster, Eldersburg, Sykesville, Taneytown, Manchester, and Mount Airy with the same RN-supervised care that has made us a trusted partner in Howard County.

## Rural Care Done Right

Many home care agencies focus their operations in dense suburban areas where routing is efficient. Carroll County includes areas that require longer travel times and more intentional scheduling. We plan our Carroll County caregiving schedules to ensure reliable, timely arrivals — and our caregivers are selected for their willingness to serve the full geography of the county.

For rural families in Carroll County, the limited availability of nearby care options makes MarkoCare's willingness to serve this area especially meaningful.

## Westminster and the Surrounding Area

Westminster, the county seat, is home to Carroll County General Hospital and a robust network of senior services. MarkoCare maintains awareness of local hospital discharge planning processes and connects families to county resources when appropriate. For clients being discharged from Carroll County General, we can coordinate a smooth transition to home care.

## A Tight-Knit Community

Carroll County has a strong sense of community. Families often prefer a care agency that feels local rather than corporate — one where the person on the phone is a real coordinator, not a call center. MarkoCare is that agency. We are small enough to know our clients and large enough to provide the clinical infrastructure that keeps them safe.

## Services Available in Carroll County

All MarkoCare services are available throughout Carroll County: personal care, companion care, dementia care, respite care, post-hospital support, cancer care support, and IPOP transitional care.
