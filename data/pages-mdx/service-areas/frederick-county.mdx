Frederick County is Maryland's second largest county by area — a wide, varied region that stretches from the suburbs of the DC metropolitan corridor to rolling farmland and mountain communities in the west. MarkoCare serves Frederick County families in Frederick City, Thurmont, Brunswick, Middletown, Emmitsburg, Walkersville, and the surrounding area.

## Frederick City and the Urban Core

Frederick City is one of Maryland's most charming small cities, with a thriving downtown, strong civic infrastructure, and a growing senior population. Frederick Health Hospital anchors the county's acute care system, and MarkoCare maintains awareness of local discharge planning pathways for patients returning home from Frederick Health or other regional medical facilities.

## Rural and Mountain Communities

The western reaches of Frederick County — including Thurmont near Catoctin Mountain Park, and communities along the I-70 corridor — are home to families who often have fewer local care options. MarkoCare serves these areas with the understanding that rural home care requires flexible scheduling and caregivers who are comfortable traveling greater distances.

## Why Families in Frederick County Choose MarkoCare

For families in western Maryland, finding an agency that combines clinical rigor with genuine warmth can be difficult. Many large agencies do not regularly serve Frederick County or send caregivers who are unfamiliar with the area. MarkoCare's W2 employee model and RN oversight provide families with the professional standard they need, while our local Maryland roots mean we understand the communities we serve.

## Services Available in Frederick County

All MarkoCare services are available throughout Frederick County: personal care, companion care, dementia care, respite care, post-hospital support, cancer care support, and IPOP transitional care.
