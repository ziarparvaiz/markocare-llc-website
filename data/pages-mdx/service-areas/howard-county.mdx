Howard County is where MarkoCare began, and it remains the heart of our operations. Our office in Columbia puts us at the center of the county — close to the families we serve in Ellicott City, Laurel, Elkridge, Clarksville, and Highland. That proximity is not just geographical. Our caregivers, coordinators, and registered nurses live in Howard County. They know these communities, these neighborhoods, and the character of life here.

## A Community We Know Well

Howard County consistently ranks among the most desirable places to live in Maryland — and for good reason. The mix of planned communities in Columbia, historic neighborhoods in Ellicott City, and quieter residential areas throughout the county creates a diverse population of families and seniors, each with their own needs and preferences.

We serve all of them. Whether a client lives in a Columbia age-restricted community, an Ellicott City single-family home, or a rural setting near the county's edges, MarkoCare brings the same RN-supervised, W2 employee standard of care to their door.

## Rapid Response and Local Availability

Because Howard County is our primary service area, we maintain our fastest response times here. For urgent post-hospital discharges or time-sensitive care needs, we can often arrange same-day or next-day placement. Our RN can typically conduct an initial assessment within 24 hours of a new inquiry.

## Hospital and Healthcare Connections

Howard County General Hospital, a member of Johns Hopkins Medicine, serves as the county's primary acute care facility. MarkoCare maintains relationships with discharge planners and case managers at the hospital, enabling smooth care transitions from inpatient to home. We are also connected to local senior resource networks, area agencies on aging, and community health organizations throughout the county.

## Services Available in Howard County

All MarkoCare services are available throughout Howard County: personal care, companion care, dementia care, respite care, post-hospital support, cancer care support, and IPOP transitional care.
