Anne Arundel County stretches from the suburban corridor near Baltimore to the Chesapeake Bay waterfront, encompassing some of Maryland's most historic and diverse communities. From Annapolis — the state capital — to Glen Burnie, Severna Park, Pasadena, Crofton, and Odenton, MarkoCare serves families across this varied county with consistent, RN-supervised home care.

## Annapolis and the Capital Region

Annapolis is a city with a strong sense of history and community. Its older population includes many families who have lived in the area for decades and want to remain in their homes as long as possible. MarkoCare's home care services make that possible — providing the support that extends independence without requiring a move to a care facility.

## Suburban Communities and the Baltimore Corridor

The northern portion of Anne Arundel County — including Glen Burnie, Pasadena, and Severna Park — sits at the edge of the Baltimore metropolitan area. Families here often have complex care needs driven by proximity to major medical centers like University of Maryland Medical Center and Johns Hopkins Hospital. We coordinate with discharge planners from these facilities to ensure that Anne Arundel County patients have appropriate home care in place before they leave the hospital.

## Waterfront and Bay Communities

The Chesapeake Bay shoreline communities in Anne Arundel County have their own character — quieter, more spread out, and sometimes less accessible to standard home care agencies. MarkoCare serves these areas and understands the geography well enough to schedule effectively.

## Services Available in Anne Arundel County

All MarkoCare services are available throughout Anne Arundel County: personal care, companion care, dementia care, respite care, post-hospital support, cancer care support, and IPOP transitional care.
