When a discharge planner, social worker, or physician refers a patient to a home care agency, they are placing their professional trust in that agency. They are telling a vulnerable person: this organization will take care of you when you leave our facility. That is not a small thing.

MarkoCare takes referral partnerships seriously because we understand what is at stake. When you send a patient to us, we treat that referral as a commitment — to the patient, to their family, and to you.

## What We Offer Referring Clinicians

**Rapid intake response.** For urgent referrals, we target a response within hours — not days. Our intake coordinator prioritizes time-sensitive discharges and communicates directly with the referring party throughout the placement process.

**Registered nurse oversight.** Every MarkoCare client is assessed by our RN, who develops a care plan aligned with the clinical information provided at the time of referral. If you have specific care instructions or treatment protocols you need followed, our RN is the right person to coordinate with.

**W2 employee caregivers.** Unlike agencies that place independent contractors, every MarkoCare caregiver is a W2 employee — trained, insured, background-checked, and held to our clinical standards. You are not referring your patient to an unknown contractor. You are referring them to our team.

**Closed-loop communication.** We provide progress updates to referring clinicians when relevant and maintain a communication channel for questions or concerns about a patient's care.

## Our HIPAA Commitment

All referrals are handled through HIPAA-compliant processes. We accept referrals via our secure online form, fax, or direct phone contact with our intake coordinator. Patient information is handled with the same confidentiality standards we apply to all client data.
