Not every career gives you the opportunity to genuinely change someone's life — to be the person who makes an elderly widow feel less alone, who helps a stroke survivor regain their morning routine, who sits with a family navigating a dementia diagnosis and makes them feel less afraid.

Caregiving does all of that. And at MarkoCare, we build our entire organization around the people who do this work.

## What We Believe About Our Team

We believe that caregiving is skilled work that deserves to be compensated fairly, supported clinically, and recognized genuinely. Too many home care agencies treat caregivers as interchangeable labor. We do not operate that way.

When we hire a caregiver, we are making a commitment: to train them well, back them up with registered nurse support, pay them fairly, give them a consistent schedule, and treat them with the same respect and dignity that we ask them to extend to their clients.

## A Culture Built on Trust

The MarkoCare culture starts with one principle: caregivers who feel supported provide better care. That means our supervisors are responsive, not distant. Our RN is available for clinical questions, not just oversight. Our coordinators are problem-solvers, not gatekeepers.

If something is not working — a scheduling issue, a client concern, a difficult situation at a home — our team addresses it quickly and collaboratively. We do not leave caregivers to figure things out alone.

## Growth and Development

Joining MarkoCare is not a dead end. We invest in ongoing training — including specialized education in dementia care, cancer support, post-hospital transitions, and infection prevention — because we believe that growing our team's capabilities makes us better for clients and more fulfilling for caregivers.

We recognize performance. We promote from within when the opportunity exists. We want our caregivers to build careers with us, not just fill shifts.

## Who We Are Looking For

We are looking for people who care — genuinely, deeply. Clinical skills can be developed and refined. Empathy, reliability, and character are what we hire for first.

If you are the kind of person who takes pride in showing up for someone who needs you, we want to hear from you.
