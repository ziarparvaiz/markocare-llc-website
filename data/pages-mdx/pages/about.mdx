MarkoCare was built from a simple and deeply personal realization: finding home care that is both clinically sound and genuinely warm is harder than it should be.

Families navigating aging, illness, or disability often find themselves choosing between agencies that offer clinical oversight without humanity, or warmth without rigor. We were founded to eliminate that false choice — to prove that professional, RN-supervised home care and genuine compassion are not competing values. They are the same thing, done right.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for every client we serve by providing compassionate, dignified, and skilled home care — empowering individuals to live safely and independently in the comfort of their own homes.

That language reflects a clinical commitment and a human one. Every word is intentional. "Enhance" — not just maintain. "Dignified" — not just functional. "Empower" — not just manage.

## The RN Backbone

<RnSupervision />

The clinical cornerstone of MarkoCare is registered nurse supervision. Every client care plan is developed by our RN — not a coordinator, not an administrator, but a licensed clinician who understands health conditions, functional assessments, and safety risks.

The RN does not disappear after intake. She makes regular supervisory home visits, monitors for changes in the client's condition, communicates with physicians and family members, and adjusts care plans as needs evolve. This is the standard of care we believe every family deserves — and what sets MarkoCare apart from most private-duty agencies in Maryland.

## Why We Chose the W2 Model

<W2Model />

When we designed MarkoCare, we made a deliberate decision to hire caregivers as W2 employees rather than independent contractors. This choice costs more. It requires more administrative infrastructure. And it was completely non-negotiable.

W2 employment means our caregivers are trained to MarkoCare standards — not self-trained to their own. It means they are covered by our liability insurance and workers' compensation — not exposing your family to gaps in coverage. It means they are accountable to our clinical team and subject to our performance standards.

When a MarkoCare caregiver enters your home, they represent our agency in every sense of the word.
