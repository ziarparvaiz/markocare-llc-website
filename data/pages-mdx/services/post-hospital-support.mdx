The period immediately following hospitalization is among the highest-risk windows in a person's care journey. Studies show that a significant proportion of hospital readmissions occur within the first 30 days after discharge — and the majority are preventable with adequate support at home. MarkoCare's post-hospital support program is built around closing this gap.

## Why Home Recovery Requires Active Support

Hospital discharge does not mean recovery is complete. It means recovery continues at home — often in an environment that has not been assessed for safety, by a patient managing multiple new medications, with a care plan that can be difficult for families to understand and implement alone.

Our RN-supervised approach addresses each of these dimensions directly. We coordinate with the hospital discharge team before the patient leaves, assess the home environment on arrival, reconcile medications, and put a structured care plan in place from day one.

## Coordination With Your Medical Team

We do not operate in isolation from your other providers. MarkoCare works alongside home health agencies, physical therapists, occupational therapists, physicians, and specialists to ensure that every member of your loved one's care team is aligned. Our RN communicates proactively, documents observations, and escalates concerns before they become emergencies.

## For Families Navigating Discharge

Hospital discharge often happens quickly and with more information than a family can absorb in the moment. If you are feeling overwhelmed by medication instructions, follow-up appointment schedules, or wound care instructions, our care coordinator and RN are here to help you understand what needs to happen and to make sure it does.

You do not have to figure this out alone.

## Referral Partners

If you are a hospital discharge planner, social worker, or case manager looking for a reliable home care partner for your patients, we welcome the opportunity to work with you. We offer 24-hour intake response and maintain a direct communication channel with referring clinicians. Visit our referral partners page or contact our intake team directly.

Post-hospital support is available across Howard, Carroll, Anne Arundel, and Frederick Counties, Maryland.
