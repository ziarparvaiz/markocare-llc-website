Family caregiving is one of the most selfless roles a person can take on. It is also one of the most physically and emotionally demanding. The research on caregiver burnout is clear: without adequate rest and time away from the caregiving role, the caregiver's own health deteriorates — and the quality of care they provide to their loved one inevitably suffers.

Respite care is not a failure of commitment. It is a recognition that sustainable caregiving requires recovery. MarkoCare's respite services exist to make that recovery possible.

## Planned and Emergency Respite

We offer both scheduled and emergency respite coverage. If you have planned time away — a vacation, medical appointment, work obligation, or simply a day to rest — we can arrange consistent, familiar care for your loved one in advance. If an emergency arises and you need coverage quickly, our intake team will work urgently to find a solution.

## Continuity of Care

A respite assignment is not handed off to a random caregiver. Our team briefs the respite caregiver thoroughly — reviewing the client's care plan, daily routines, preferences, and any health considerations the caregiver needs to know. When possible, we match the client with a caregiver they have met before. The goal is that your loved one's experience of care is consistent and comfortable, whether you are home or away.

## A Note to Family Caregivers

If you are reading this page, you are probably someone who has been putting their own needs last for a very long time. We want you to know something plainly: you deserve rest. Your health matters. Your peace of mind matters. Taking a break is not abandonment — it is the wisest thing you can do for your loved one and for yourself.

We will be there. Your loved one is in good hands.

Respite care is available across Howard, Carroll, Anne Arundel, and Frederick Counties, Maryland.
