## Our Commitment to Non-Discrimination

MarkoCare LLC does not discriminate in the provision of services, employment practices, or any other aspect of our operations on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, marital status, source of income, or any other characteristic protected by federal, state, or local law.

This commitment applies equally to:

- Client intake and service enrollment
- Care coordination and caregiver assignment
- Scheduling and service delivery
- Billing and payment practices
- Employment recruitment, hiring, and promotion
- All other business operations

## Federal Nondiscrimination Laws

MarkoCare complies with all applicable federal civil rights laws, including:

- **Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964** — prohibits discrimination based on race, color, and national origin in programs receiving federal financial assistance.
- **Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973** — prohibits discrimination based on disability in programs receiving federal financial assistance.
- **The Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (ADA)** — prohibits discrimination against individuals with disabilities in all areas of public life.
- **The Age Discrimination Act of 1975** — prohibits discrimination based on age in programs receiving federal financial assistance.
- **Section 1557 of the Affordable Care Act** — prohibits discrimination in health programs and activities on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, age, or disability.

## Maryland State Law

MarkoCare also complies with the Maryland Fair Employment Practices Act and all applicable Maryland anti-discrimination regulations, which prohibit discrimination in employment and service delivery based on protected characteristics under state law.

## Accessibility & Accommodation

MarkoCare is committed to providing full and equal access to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We offer:

- **Communication assistance** — We can provide written materials in alternative formats upon request.
- **Language access services** — If you require interpreter services or translated documents, please contact us and we will make reasonable arrangements to accommodate your language needs.
- **Physical accessibility** — Our office and service delivery practices are designed to be accessible to individuals with mobility or physical limitations.

If you require an accommodation to access our services, please contact us as soon as possible so we can make appropriate arrangements.

## Non-Discrimination in Care

Every individual we serve has the right to receive compassionate, dignified, and equitable care. MarkoCare caregivers are trained to provide respectful, culturally sensitive care to all clients regardless of background, identity, or circumstances.

We do not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or disrespectful treatment of clients, families, or employees by anyone acting on behalf of MarkoCare.

## Non-Discrimination in Employment

MarkoCare is an equal opportunity employer. We recruit, hire, train, promote, and compensate employees without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other characteristic protected by law.

We are committed to building a workforce that reflects the diversity of the communities we serve and that upholds the values of equity, inclusion, and dignity.
