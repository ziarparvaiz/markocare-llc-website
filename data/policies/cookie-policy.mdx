## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, improve your experience, and enable certain features. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

## 2. How MarkoCare Uses Cookies

The MarkoCare website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core features such as navigation, form submission, and security. You cannot opt out of strictly necessary cookies as the site would not function without them.

### Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you have made — such as your preferences or previously entered information — to provide a more personalized experience.

### Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This data helps us improve site performance, identify popular content, and enhance the overall user experience. Analytics data is collected in aggregated, anonymized form.

Examples include page visit counts, session duration, and navigation paths.

### Marketing & Tracking Cookies

We may use marketing cookies to deliver relevant content and measure the effectiveness of our outreach efforts. These cookies track browsing activity across websites. We do not use cookies to target individuals based on health information or to build profiles for third-party advertising.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services we use, such as:

- **Google Analytics** — for website usage analysis
- **Google Fonts** — for rendering custom typefaces
- **Form services** — for processing contact and inquiry forms

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review them independently.

## 4. How to Manage Cookies

You have the right to accept, decline, or manage cookies at any time. You can control cookie settings through:

- **Browser settings** — Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings or preferences menus.
- **Opt-out tools** — Tools such as the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on allow you to prevent analytics data collection.
- **Device settings** — Mobile devices often include settings to limit ad tracking.

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality and performance of the website.

### Managing Cookies by Browser

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Other Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions → Cookies and Site Data

## 5. Cookie Retention

Different cookies are retained for different durations:

- **Session cookies** expire when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies** remain for a fixed period (typically 30 days to 2 years) or until manually deleted
- **Analytics cookies** are typically retained for up to 26 months

## 6. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. We respect DNT signals where technically feasible. However, because there is no universally accepted standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals, our website may not respond to all DNT browser settings.
