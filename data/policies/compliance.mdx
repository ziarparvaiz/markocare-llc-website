MarkoCare is committed to operating with full transparency, regulatory compliance, and the highest ethical standards — in every interaction, in every home, every day.

## Maryland RSA Licensure

MarkoCare is actively pursuing licensure as a **Residential Service Agency (RSA)** with the Maryland Department of Health, Office of Health Care Quality (OHCQ), pursuant to **COMAR 10.07.14**.

We will not represent ourselves as licensed until licensure is officially granted. All pre-launch operations are conducted in compliance with applicable federal and state law. Upon receiving our license, this page will be updated to reflect our licensed status.

For questions about our licensure process, please contact us directly.

## Regulatory Oversight

## Client Bill of Rights

Every client receiving services from MarkoCare has the following rights, consistent with Maryland RSA regulations and applicable federal law:

- To be treated with courtesy, respect, and dignity at all times.
- To receive care that is free from abuse, neglect, and exploitation.
- To be informed of the scope and limitations of services before care begins.
- To participate in developing and modifying your care plan.
- To refuse any service or treatment at any time.
- To have your personal information kept confidential.
- To be informed of any changes to your care team or schedule in advance.
- To voice concerns or complaints without fear of retaliation.
- To have complaints reviewed and resolved in a timely manner.
- To be informed of applicable costs and billing practices.
- To receive a copy of your service agreement before services begin.
- To have access to your records in accordance with applicable law.

## Complaint Process

MarkoCare takes all client concerns and complaints seriously. We are committed to resolving issues promptly and transparently.

### Step 1 — Contact Our Care Coordinator

Call **(240) 432-4374** or email **hello@markocare.com** to report your concern. All complaints are documented and treated confidentially.

### Step 2 — Complaint Review

A supervisor or administrator will review your complaint within **2 business days** and contact you to discuss the situation and proposed resolution.

### Step 3 — Resolution & Follow-Up

We will work to resolve the complaint and provide a written summary of findings and actions taken within **10 business days** of the initial report.

### Step 4 — External Reporting

Clients also have the right to file complaints directly with the **Maryland Department of Health Office of Health Care Quality** at any time — without first contacting MarkoCare — and without fear of retaliation.

**OHCQ Complaint Hotline:** 410-402-8201
