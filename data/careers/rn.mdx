## Overview

This is a leadership role for a Registered Nurse who wants to have real clinical influence in a community-based care setting. As MarkoCare's supervising RN, you will be responsible for ensuring the safety, quality, and continuity of care for every client we serve.

You will conduct home assessments, build individualized care plans, and serve as the clinical anchor for our caregiver team — providing guidance, supervision, and support across our Maryland service area.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct initial and ongoing client home assessments
- Develop, document, and update individualized care plans
- Supervise Home Care Aides and CNAs in accordance with Maryland RSA regulations
- Perform supervisory home visits at required intervals
- Respond to clinical questions and changes in client condition
- Coordinate with families, physicians, and discharge planners as needed
- Maintain accurate clinical documentation and compliance records
- Support new caregiver orientation from a clinical standpoint

## Requirements

- Active Maryland RN license in good standing
- Minimum 2 years of clinical nursing experience
- Home health, community health, or case management experience strongly preferred
- Demonstrated ability to develop and document individualized care plans
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation for home visits
- Comfortable working independently and managing a flexible schedule

## What We Offer

- A clinically meaningful role with direct impact on client care quality
- Collaborative relationship with agency leadership and care coordinators
- Flexible part-time schedule built around your availability
- Opportunity to shape clinical standards and training for our care team
- Competitive compensation reflecting your licensure and experience

## Service Area

This role covers clients and caregiver supervision across Howard County, Carroll County, Anne Arundel County, and Frederick County, Maryland.
