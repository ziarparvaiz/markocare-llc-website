## Overview

As a Certified Nursing Assistant at MarkoCare, you bring clinical skill and genuine compassion to clients who need more than basic assistance. You will work closely with our supervising RN to deliver care that is safe, dignified, and centered around each client's individual needs.

This is a meaningful role for a CNA who wants to work in a home-based setting — where the pace allows you to truly know your clients and make a sustained difference in their lives.

## Responsibilities

- Perform skilled personal care tasks within CNA scope of practice
- Assist with bathing, dressing, grooming, oral care, and transfers
- Take and record vital signs as directed by the supervising RN
- Support safe mobility, positioning, and fall prevention
- Provide companionship and report behavioral or cognitive changes
- Complete accurate and timely care documentation
- Communicate effectively with the care team, client, and family

## Requirements

- Active Maryland CNA certification in good standing
- Current CPR certification
- Experience in a home care, assisted living, or skilled nursing setting preferred
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to pass a comprehensive state and federal background check
- Current TB test result (or willingness to obtain)
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation

## What We Offer

- Competitive W2 hourly compensation that reflects your certification and experience
- Flexible scheduling — part-time and full-time shifts available
- Ongoing training in dementia care, cancer support, and post-hospital transitions
- Close collaboration with and direct access to our supervising RN
- A supportive team culture where clinical staff are valued and heard

## Service Area

This position serves clients in Howard County, Carroll County, Anne Arundel County, and Frederick County, Maryland.
