## Overview

At MarkoCare, our Home Care Aides are the foundation of the care we deliver. You are the one showing up — day after day — to help a client stay safely and comfortably in the home they love. That matters more than we can put into words.

We are looking for dependable, warm-hearted individuals who want more than just a job. If you take pride in the work you do, enjoy building real relationships with the people you serve, and want to be part of a team that genuinely supports you — this role is for you.

## Responsibilities

- Assist clients with activities of daily living (bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting)
- Provide companionship and emotional support
- Help with light housekeeping, laundry, and meal preparation
- Support safe mobility and transfers
- Monitor and report changes in client condition to the supervising RN
- Accompany clients to appointments and errands as needed
- Document care activities accurately and on time

## Requirements

- High school diploma or GED
- Minimum 1 year of caregiving experience preferred (family caregiving counts)
- CPR certification, or willingness to obtain before start date
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
- Ability to pass a comprehensive state and federal background check
- Compassionate, patient, and professional demeanor

## What We Offer

- Competitive W2 hourly compensation with regular performance reviews
- Flexible scheduling — part-time, full-time, and live-in options
- Paid orientation and ongoing specialized training at no cost to you
- Direct RN supervision and clinical support on every assignment
- A team culture that treats you with respect every day

## Service Area

This position serves clients in Howard County, Carroll County, Anne Arundel County, and Frederick County, Maryland.
